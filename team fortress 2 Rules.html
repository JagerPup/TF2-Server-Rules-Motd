TEAM FORTRESS 2 SERVER RULES

Please follow all rules to keep the server fun, balanced, and enjoyable for everyone.

Community Rules

  1. Respect all players and staff members.
  2. No racism, hate speech, sexism, or discrimination.
  3. No harassment, bullying, or targeted toxicity.
  4. Do not intentionally ruin another player's experience.
  5. Do not shame players for skill level or class choice.
  6. New players should be treated respectfully.
  7. No impersonating admins or other players.
  8. No excessive toxicity or rage baiting.
  9. No personal information leaks or doxxing.
  10. Keep usernames, sprays, and profile pictures appropriate.

Gameplay Rules

  1. No cheating, hacking, or scripting.
  2. No exploiting glitches, bugs, or unintended mechanics.
  3. No AFK farming or idle abuse.
  4. No spawn camping unless naturally allowed by the map.
  5. No intentionally feeding the enemy team.
  6. No griefing teammates or sabotaging objectives.
  7. Objective gameplay is encouraged.
  8. Friendly players must move during active objectives.
  9. No excessive round delaying.
  10. Do not intentionally block teammates.

Communication Rules

  1. No mic spam or earrape audio.
  2. Push-to-talk is strongly recommended.
  3. No excessive chat spam.
  4. No advertising other communities or servers.
  5. English only in public chat unless otherwise allowed.
  6. No political or controversial arguments.
  7. No fake vote kicks or vote abuse.
  8. False reports against players or staff are prohibited.
  9. Keep memes and trolling under control.
  10. No baiting admins or moderators.

Performance & Stability Rules

  1. No lag exploit abuse.
  2. No command flooding or bind spam.
  3. No particle or unusual effect abuse to cause lag.
  4. No server crash attempts.
  5. No reconnect exploit abuse.
  6. No inventory crash exploits.
  7. No sound exploit abuse.
  8. Do not intentionally reduce server performance.
  9. Report major bugs to staff immediately.
  10. Attempting to bypass restrictions is punishable.

Class & Weapon Rules

  1. No exploiting broken server weapons or plugins.
  2. Engineers may not grief with teleporters or buildings.
  3. Pyros may not intentionally airblast teammates repeatedly.
  4. Spies may not abuse disguise or cloak glitches.
  5. Sniper scripts or aim assistance are prohibited.
  6. Do not pocket obvious cheaters.
  7. No exploiting unusual cosmetics to crash clients.
  8. Random crit arguments must remain civil.
  9. Admins may limit overpowered setups if necessary.
  10. Gameplay balance decisions are final.

Staff & Punishment Rules

  1. Admins must follow all server rules.
  2. Admin abuse will not be tolerated.
  3. Staff decisions are final during active situations.
  4. Do not argue punishments in public chat.
  5. Ban evasion results in permanent bans.
  6. Repeated rule breaking increases punishment severity.
  7. Appeals should remain respectful and honest.
  8. Recording evidence for reports is encouraged.
  9. Threats toward players or staff are taken seriously.
  10. Staff reserve the right to punish harmful behavior not explicitly listed.

Server Success Guidelines

  • Keep gameplay fair and balanced.
  • Respect the community.
  • Help new players learn the game.
  • Avoid unnecessary drama.
  • Report cheaters and exploiters.
  • Most importantly: Have fun!
